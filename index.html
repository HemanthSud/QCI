Our Story

More Than Dance. A Family.

Queen City Ishaare is a competitive Bollywood fusion dance team at UNC Charlotte. We blend classical Indian dance with contemporary styles, hip-hop, and filmi choreography to create performances that honor culture and push artistic boundaries.

From regional competitions to campus showcases, every performance is a celebration of South Asian artistry and the Queen City spirit.
